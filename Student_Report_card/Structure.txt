### * Student Report Card System*  
*Description*: A system to manage student grades and performance.  

#### Features:  
- *Admin Panel*:  
  - Add, update, and delete student records.  
  - Assign subjects to students.  
- *Marks Management*:  
  - Input marks for exams.  
  - Calculate total marks and grades based on pre-defined criteria.  
- *Reports*:  
  - Generate report cards for individual students.  
  - View overall class performance.  

#### Database Tables:  
- students: Store student details.  
- subjects: Store subject information.  
- marks: Store marks for each subject and student.
